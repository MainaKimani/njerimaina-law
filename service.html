Services

01

Civil Litigation & Commercial Litigation

Whether the dispute is between companies or individuals, our seasoned litigators understand that being trial-ready is often the difference between winning and losing. We therefore work hard to devise strategies that ensure that the wheels of justice turn smoothly in your favor.

Our litigation strategy is client-centered and based on a thorough understanding of Civil Law Procedures and rules.

Our areas of expertise include:

  • Debt Recovery and Management
  • Insurance Claims and Settlement
  • Employment and Labour claims
  • Contractual Disputes
  • Business Disputes
  • Intellectual Property Disputes
  • Constitution and Administrative Law

02

Criminal Litigation

When things go wrong, we are the people you want by your side. We will examine the evidence against you, advise you on the appropriate plea, and inform you of the potential sentence.

At Njeri Maina Law Advocates, we believe in justice and fairness and will go above and beyond to fight for you and achieve the best possible outcomes, regardless of the nature or circumstances of your case.

03

Succession and Estate Planning

We are here to support you during the difficult times. Our professional and compassionate team is able to assist in what can be a difficult and emotional process.

Our expertise includes:

  • Application for Grant of Probate or letters of administration
  • Drafting of Wills
  • Registration of charitable Trusts
  • Preparation and Registration of Power of Attorney

04

Banking and Finance

We provide top-notch service to all of our clients, regardless of size. Our team has extensive experience in providing legal advice as well as value-added capabilities to small, medium, and large corporations.

We are proactive in anticipating key legal issues that our clients may face and how they can be addressed most efficiently and cost-effectively in order to meet our clients' business objectives.

Our services include:

  • Advisories on legal and regulatory compliance
  • Asset Finance
  • Drafting and execution of securitization documents
  • Registration of Securities

05

Conveyancing and Real Estate

Our Conveyancing and Real Estate department is well equipped and experienced in a broad variety of property-related matters.

We represent buyers, sellers, borrowers, lenders and property owners in navigating complex real estate transactions.

Our expertise includes:

  • Searches and due diligence on property
  • Sale and Transfer of land
  • Caveats, cautions, restrictions and Inhibitions
  • Charges and Mortgages
  • Subdivision and Change of user
  • Real Estate Dispute Resolution
